The AmbassaDiver Program


The AmbassaDiver program consists of our most passionate divers, dive professionals, and ocean champions who are committed to joining us in being torchbearers for the ocean planet. They are actively working towards a better balance between humanity and the ocean, both in their communities and across the planet. Their brand advocacy helps enrich a global community of engaged divers while simultaneously bringing awareness to social and environmental issues, inspiring hope in others.
